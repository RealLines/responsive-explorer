A Clear Vision from Day One

Chayse’s Cleaning started with a ladder, a dream, and a drive to deliver clarity—literally. From humble beginnings in a small neighborhood to servicing homes and storefronts across the city, our mission has remained simple: make your view as bright as possible.

Meet Chayse

In 2023, Chayse Finkelstein set out to offer honest, hard-working service with a smile. What began as a weekend side hustle quickly grew into a trusted name in window cleaning. Customers loved the streak-free shine, but they stayed for the friendliness and reliability.

Today, Chayse’s Cleaning serves both residential and commercial clients, offering customized packages and top-tier products that leave glass gleaming. We're committed to providing outstanding results and a seamless customer experience.
